About Me

i'm Joe

I'm a Full Stack Software Developer originally from Pennsylvania and now living in the Atlanta, Georgia area. I attended Penn State University and have a degree in Human Development and Family Studies, as well as some psychology and sociology. I have lived in Philadelphia and the Atlanta area working in the social services field for several years because I have a passion for understanding the issues that people and families face, helping them to empower themselves to overcome obstacles and reach their goals, and to ultimately change their lives for the better.

Eventually, I began to feel a little confined with what I saw as the rigid, uncreative methods and structure of traditional social work methods and began to try to think of ways I could approach my passion for helping and empowering others in new and innovative ways. In 2018, after speaking with some software developer friends and reading about programs that were teaching military veterans to code as a second career, I began teaching myself to code via different online tutorial websites. I realized that I loved the challenge and creativity involved in using software engineering to help people and companies reach their goals in new and constantly evolving ways. I graduated from the six month, full stack flex program at DigitalCrafts in August of 2018.
